Privacy Policy — Grocery List Helper

Last updated: July 17, 2026

This privacy policy explains what information Grocery List Helper ("the App," "we," "us") collects when you use our website (grocerylist.devkeo.com) and our Alexa skill, and how that information is used and protected.

Information We Collect

When you create an account or use the App, we may collect:

  • Username — a name you choose to identify your grocery list.
  • PIN — an optional numeric code you may set to protect access to your account. PINs are encrypted before storage.
  • Grocery list items — the names of items you add to your list, whether entered on the website or by voice through the Alexa skill.
  • Photos — if you choose to attach a photo to an item on your list, that image is stored in our database and associated with that item.
  • Rewards card numbers — if you choose to add a rewards or loyalty card number to your account, this information is encrypted before storage.

We do not collect payment information, precise location data, or health information.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to operate the App's core functionality:

  • To identify your account and keep your grocery list private to you.
  • To display your list, including any photos you've attached to items, when you log in.
  • To allow the Amazon Alexa service to add items to your list on your behalf, once you have linked your Amazon account to your Grocery List Helper account.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. We do not use your data to build advertising profiles.

Alexa Account Linking

If you choose to link your Amazon account with Grocery List Helper through the Alexa app, we use an industry-standard OAuth 2.0 authorization process. This allows the Alexa service to securely add items to your grocery list on your behalf when you use voice commands. Linking only grants the ability to add items associated with your existing Grocery List Helper account — it does not give Alexa or Amazon access to your PIN, rewards card numbers, or any other account information.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using Supabase, a secure hosted database provider. PINs and rewards card numbers are encrypted before being stored. Access to the underlying database is restricted to the App's server-side systems only.

Children's Privacy

Grocery List Helper is not directed to, and does not knowingly collect personal information from, children under the age of 13.

Your Choices

You may update or delete the items, photos, and rewards card information associated with your account at any time through the website. If you would like your account and all associated data permanently deleted, contact us using the information below.

You may unlink your Amazon account from Grocery List Helper at any time through the Alexa app (Skills & Games → Your Skills → Grocery List Helper → Disable Skill).

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
